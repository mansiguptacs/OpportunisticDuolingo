What it is

Turn browsing into a living neural atlas.

Synapse maps concepts from articles and tabs onto a 3D brain, then pulls you back with witty nudges and a guided review path when mastery starts to fade.

  • Capture Alchemize any page via the Chrome extension into teachable concepts.
  • Explore Neural, Graph, and Garden views show how ideas connect — and dry out.
  • Revise Today’s path: teach → probe → synthesis quiz before concepts go overdue.

Built on Butterbase (vault + Edge SSR), EverOS (agent memory), and Grok (teach copy & nudge tone) — live at synapse.butterbase.dev.
